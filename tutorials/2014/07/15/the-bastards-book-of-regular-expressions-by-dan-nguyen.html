The Bastards Book of Regular Expressions by Dan Nguyen

The Bastards Book of Regular Expressions by Dan Nguyen

I love regexes even more because they make it possible – if not trivial – to do research and analysis that is otherwise impossible, because believe it or not, most research and analysis start out with just giant blobs of text
The Bastard’s Book of Regex is a great (free!) text on cleaning up crappy data with regular expressions. Given this is the work of Dan Nguyen, there is definitely a nod to cleaning OCR’d and scraped data in there.
